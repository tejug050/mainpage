Office Best Practices

Do's

1. Timely Attendance

  • Every employee should clock in at 9:00 AM and clock out at 5:30 PM.

2. Energy Conservation

  • Turn off lights, fans, and close office doors when leaving the premises.

3. Professional Appearance

  • All employees should maintain a professional appearance through their attire.

4. Cleanliness

  • Keep the surroundings clean and utilize waste bins for proper waste disposal.

5. Team Support

  • Support each other in accomplishing tasks and reaching goals.

Don'ts

1. Skipping Attendance Procedures

  • Don't neglect to log in at 9:00 AM and log out at 5:30 PM as per company policy.

2. Ignoring Energy Conservation

  • Avoid leaving lights, fans, or office equipment on when leaving the office. Always ensure to switch them off.

3. Neglecting Appearance Standards

  • Don't disregard the company's dress code policy. Maintain a professional appearance with appropriate attire.

4. Overlooking Cleanliness

  • Avoid leaving your workspace untidy. Dispose of waste properly in designated bins and maintain cleanliness.

5. Lacking Team Support

  • Don't hesitate to lend a helping hand to your colleagues. Support each other in achieving work objectives and deadlines.
