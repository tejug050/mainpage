About QA Position

We are seeking a skilled QA Engineer to join our product development team. As a QA Engineer at Omnitech Solutions, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our networking and embedded products.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Good understanding of networking protocols such as TCP/IP, UDP, HTTPS, SSL, Ethernet, and WiFi.
  • Experience in testing using tools like TCP dump, Wireshark, and Sniffers, with proficiency in Linux for Test Automation.
  • Hands-on experience in testing various networking products like Switches and Routers.
  • Experience with DPDK-based products.
  • Understanding of API testing, including Rest/JSON testing.
  • Proficiency in writing scripts using Linux shell scripts or Python.

Qualifications:

Minimum qualification: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electronics & Communication, or equivalent, with at least 1 year of relevant experience.
